How To Play

Once you click "Start Game", the simulation will score you on the correctiveness on 10 offside decisions. The offside decisions will simulate only after your choice on Offside or Onside. Each offside play will be played once you click "Run". There is also a difficulty button to adjust the skill level (default level is Medium) and a button to reset the simulation.
